Here's Who I am.

My Name is Erick Atanga. I feel like one word cannot really describe who I am or what my interests are; this is because I am interested in numerous things. I like to draw (whether it is photorealistic, or cartoon). I've learned how to design and develop websites, whether by hand, or through CMSs like WordPress. I also create animations using the Adobe Suite, and I have experience in mobile app development (utilizing Java and Android Studio, for instance).

If I could pick one word to describe me, I'd say that I am a designer. I've never stuck to one type of niche. From as young as the age 5, I would draw concept designs for consumer electronics like computers and video game consoles, illustrate famous cartoon characters, make comics, and attempt to make my own animations using Windows Movie Maker and MS Paint. I even love to look at what has already been made and improve on it. I have an eye for design, and I have the ambition to see this creativity through.

This website - which I built from scratch utilizing multiple programming languages (HTML, CSS, JavaScript/JQuery, PHP) and graphic design software - is dedicated to showing off the types of content I create - both personal projects, work projects. You'll see a variety of creations (that will continue to grow as I update it), from multiple mediums, each with a unique and distinct style in my portfolio. If you would like to purchase or support me, contact me. Thanks!
